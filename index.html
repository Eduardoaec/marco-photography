About

Marco Ortiz

I am a photographer with several years of experience, specializing in concert and live event photography. My work is driven by a desire to preserve moments that would otherwise fade with time. In a constantly changing world, I see photography as a way to hold onto what truly matters.

I believe images should not only show how something looked, but how it felt. That is what guides my approach: capturing honest, fleeting moments with intention. Creating images that allow the heart to remember.
